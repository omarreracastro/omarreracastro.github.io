About Us

At A Laundromat our goal is to provide a hassle free laundry environment.
This is a self serve laundromat with seventeen washers and ten dryers available. We also provide a reliable drop off service.
If you would like to get your clothes washed in a safe and clean environment stop by and see us.
We are located at 180 Muldoon Road Anchorage, Ak. (Muldoon and Peck Ave)
Our hours of operation are 8am to 9:30pm Monday - Thursday and 8:30am to 11:00pm Friday - Sunday.
Come in today we would love to see you!
